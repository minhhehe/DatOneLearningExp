The phophercy foretold that eventually a group of adventurers, consisted of various backgrounds, would emerge, venture into the land of darkness, discover the secret behind the ruins and save the world from the clutch of Darkness...

Name: Athene
Race: Human
Weapon: Lance
About: The daughter of Viscountess Metis - head of a famous military family in the center of Gaia. Extremely strong with her heirloom weapon, the Lance Gungnir.
She had a sparring partner and a dear friend named Snow, together they trained and grew closer to each other, however, when Snow revealed herself to be the Lord of the Demon Race, many humans shunned her for their misfortunes, except Athene.
Unfortunately, Athene's affection alone could not stop Snow from being corrupted by Uma's energy and manevolent's wills around her and one day she disappeared...
After the disappearance of Snow, Athene started seeking for a way to find Snow and the rumour that Snow has appeared near the ruin of Voskara reached Athene's ears.
"I will challenge you with all that I have. Today, I will prove that I have surpassed you!""
